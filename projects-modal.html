WelTec remake
August 2022

After seeing how well the recreation of Mount Victoria turned out, I decided to remake a more convenient location. This is an unfinished render of a classroom at WelTec, created with Autodesk Maya and Pixar RenderMan. The scene extends about 50 metres in the distance, revealing some of the council building. I had several ideas for ways to extend this project, for instance using it in a short film or a video game, or just a background for product renders.

Mount Victoria remake
June 2022

This assignment required making a 3D environment using photogrammetry techniques. Naturally I took things a bit further by creating new geometry using the scan as reference. This render features technologies such as nCloth for the clothes and xGen for the grass. The ground texture comes directly from the scanned model. Due to time limits I wasn't able to create the trees in the background, so the environment appears to cut away into the void.

Major Projects Game
June 2022

For Major Projects semester 1, I thought about creating a simple exploration game that would serve as a manifestation of my other work. Each area would be thematically-relevant, for instance, my photography work would be presented in an art gallery, while the Cyberthug Core would be a life-size walkthrough area complete with visual and aural effects.


I had planned to extend the project by creating a handheld game console based on the Raspberry Pi complete with its own custom-built operating system that would resemble the Nintendo DS, but due to time limits I never got around to it.
