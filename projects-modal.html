Te Auaha remake
August 2022

After seeing how well the recreations of the Victoria University law school and Mount Victoria turned out, I decided to remake a more convenient location. The image above is an unfinished render of a classroom at the Dixon St campus, created with Autodesk Maya and Pixar RenderMan.


The scene extends about 50 metres in the distance, revealing some of the council building. I had several ideas for ways to extend this project, for instance using it in a short film or a video game, or just a background for product renders.
